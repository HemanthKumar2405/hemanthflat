3BHK Flat

A 3BHK flat offers a perfect balance of space and comfort, ideal for families. With three bedrooms, a hall, and a kitchen, it provides ample room for living and storage. Modern layouts often include attached bathrooms and balconies, enhancing the living experience. These flats are popular in urban areas, combining luxury and convenience.

2BHK Flat

A 2BHK flat features two bedrooms, a hall, and a kitchen, making it a compact yet comfortable option for small families or couples. It offers an efficient use of space while maintaining modern amenities. Often designed with attached bathrooms and open living areas, it strikes a balance between affordability and functionality. Ideal for urban living.

4BHK Flat

A 4BHK flat provides expansive living space with four bedrooms, a hall, and a kitchen, perfect for larger families or those seeking luxury. It often includes multiple bathrooms, balconies, and dedicated areas for storage or study. These flats offer enhanced privacy and comfort, with spacious layouts designed for premium living. Ideal for those desiring ample room and upscale amenities.
